## Phase 1: Rewrite Core Copy

**Goal:** Transform the homepage from a generic "technology executive" introduction into a sharp, board-facing narrative that leads with AI enterprise enablement and names specific revenue and operational outcomes.

**Requirements covered:** HERO-01, HERO-02, HERO-03, IMPA-01, IMPA-02, IMPA-03

**Success criteria:**
1. A board member reading the hero section in 15 seconds understands Ryan builds AI programs *and* drives org-wide adoption — not just architecture
2. The hero H1 contains no jargon that requires a footnote ("ecosystems", "resilient", "intelligent")
3. The Pulte impact card names at least one AI-specific outcome tied to revenue or efficiency
4. The "Operating system" section leads with AI enablement language before technical tooling
5. No badge says just "Cloud transformation" without an AI-connected framing

**Plans:**
1. Hero section rewrite (eyebrow, H1, intro)
2. Signature impact card update (Pulte AI story)
3. Operating system section reframe (AI enablement first, technical craft second)
